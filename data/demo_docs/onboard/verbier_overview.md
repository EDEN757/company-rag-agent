The Verbier Q4 ski retreat is the company's annual offsite for 2025.
Dates: 2025-12-08 through 2025-12-11. Location: Hotel Sundial,
Verbier, Switzerland. The goal of the retreat is cross-team bonding
and on-snow ski lessons for new hires who joined since the previous
offsite. Roughly 52 employees are invited. The program is owned by
morgan@acmeco.io. Cancellation deadline for the venue is 2025-10-15.
