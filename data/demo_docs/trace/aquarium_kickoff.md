On 2025-08-12, jamie@acmeco.io proposed installing a 200-litre saltwater
aquarium in the Zurich office lobby as a wellbeing initiative. The
proposal was approved the same day by morgan@acmeco.io. Vendor:
Aqua-Marine Zürich GmbH. Tank type: reef tank with live coral.
Scheduled installation window: 2025-08-25 to 2025-08-28. Owner of the
project: jamie@acmeco.io. Slack channel for updates: #office-aquarium.
