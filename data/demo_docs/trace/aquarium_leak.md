shift at the Zurich office. Between 22:30 on 2025-09-14 and 06:00 on

its sump compartment. Approximately three square metres of lobby
carpet were soaked. ops@aqua-marine.example was contacted at 06:30 and
arrived on-site at 09:15 to drain the tank and inspect the cabinet.
Priority: P1. Owner: jamie@acmeco.io.
