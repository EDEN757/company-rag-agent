Resolution writeup of the saltwater aquarium leak incident
(see Jira AQUA-7). On 2025-09-22 Aqua-Marine Zürich replaced the
faulty sump and reinforced the cabinet base with a stainless tray.
On 2025-09-26 they confirmed in writing that the carpet replacement
(CHF 1,800) is covered under the installation warranty signed on
2025-08-25. Reef tank and live coral were re-introduced on
2025-09-28 with no further leaks observed. Project owner
jamie@acmeco.io closed AQUA-7 on 2025-09-28.
